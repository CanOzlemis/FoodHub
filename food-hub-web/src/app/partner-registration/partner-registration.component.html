Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

What is a partner?

A partner is the restaurants that are registered within the system

How to become a partner?

If you own a restaurant and want to become partners with us, you just need to fill the form below! The first chance someone from Food Hub will get in contact with you to prove the legitamacy of your request to become a partner. Once the legitamacy is proven, your profile will be created using the details you supplied in the form with the access for your own restaurant will be created in the system and you will be mailed the login details.

How much does it cost to be a partner?

Nothing. It is FREE to be partners with us.

What is the benefit of being partner with Food Hub?

Food Hub delivers management system to the restaurants allowing for easy custimisation of menu's, restaurant details, working times etc. Together with this, the partners have access to order management systems allowing for easy way of dealing and tracking their orders with maximum efficiency. Restaurants can track their own performances with easy to understand charts to see and maximise their profits. The feedback system allows for restaurants to get feedback from their customers in order to improve their dishes, service, presentation etc. Doesn't matter how big or small the restaurant is, every partner in the system is given a fair competition.
